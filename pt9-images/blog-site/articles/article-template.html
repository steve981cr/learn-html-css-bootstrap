Image Examples

You can add images to your article.

  • Round corners class="rounded"
  • Make the image responsive class="img-fluid"
  • Center image horizontally - surround it in a div with class="text-center". "Text-end" aligns it to the right.
  • To add a caption to the bottom of the image, place a figcaption element below the img, and place both within a figure element.
  • To center the caption class="figure-caption text-center"
  • Align image to the left and wrap other elements to the right of it class="float-start"
  • Align image to the right and wrap other elements to the left of it class="float-end"
